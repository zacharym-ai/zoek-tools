Industry CPL benchmarks

What's a "good" cost per lead depends on your industry and client value. These are typical ranges Zoek sees across our 50,000+ active clients — use as a gut-check, not gospel.

Home services
Plumbing / HVAC / Electrical
$30–$80per lead

High competition on Google. Reviews and referrals are critical differentiators.

Legal services
Law Firms
$50–$300per lead

Wide range by practice area. High client LTV justifies the premium.

Healthcare
Medical / Dental / Clinics
$25–$100per lead

Patient LTV makes higher CPLs sustainable. Local SEO is highest-ROI channel here.

Real estate
Agents & Brokerages
$20–$120per lead

Close rate is the real variable. At 2–5% close, even $100/lead is profitable.

E-commerce
Online Retail / DTC
$5–$30per lead

Email capture costs are lower but conversion and retention matter most. Think ROAS.

B2B / SaaS
Software & Services
$40–$200per lead

LinkedIn tends to be expensive but high-quality. High LTV contracts justify premium CPLs.
